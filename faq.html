FAQ

Pulsepress is a platform providing access to a curated selection of sports nutrition magazines and exciting sports-related games.

Users can subscribe to Pulsepress on a daily or weekly basis by confirming the subscription on the mobile site. The subscription grants access to the platform, enabling users read top sport and nutrition magazines.

Subscribers will gain access to a diverse range of content, including expert articles on sports nutrition, workout routines, athlete interviews, healthy recipes, and the latest trends in the sports and fitness industry.

Subscribers can cancel their subscription at any time by accessing their personal menu and clicking on the "Unsubscribe" link. The cancellation will prevent auto-renewal, and users will maintain access until the end of the current subscription period.

Currently, Pulsepress does not offer a free trial. However, users can explore the platform's features and benefits by subscribing for a daily or weekly plan.

We take user privacy seriously. Personal data is handled in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. Users have the right to access, rectify, and/or oppose any personal data by contacting the data controller at NEXT MOVE DIGITAL.

You will keep your access to Pulsepress until the last period paid ends. For example, in case of daily subscription which was already renewed today, you will be able to read all magazines until current date ends. Or if you were subscribed to weekly and your last renewal happened on Monday, you will have access until Sunday (7 days).
